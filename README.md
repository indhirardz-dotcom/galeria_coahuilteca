Galería Coahuilteca

A working gallery in San Miguel de Allende presenting a focused selection of original works by local artists.

This is not a souvenir space.
All pieces are unique and intended for collectors.

What is collected here

Paintings, drawings and ceramic pieces developed through direct engagement with material, process and lived experience.

The gallery operates as both studio and exhibition space, allowing visitors to encounter the work at different stages of creation.

Artists

Fermigrante
Acrylic paintings centered on human density, movement and collective presence.
His work is among the most acquired in the gallery.

Indhira
Figurative drawing and painting exploring identity, the body and personal narrative through a technically rigorous approach.
Includes oil painting in Venetian technique.

For collectors

Most works are created on paper or lightweight formats that can be safely rolled and transported.

Shipping options available.

Collectors are encouraged to request available pieces directly.
