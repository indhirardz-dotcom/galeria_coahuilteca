Portrait Paintings
by Indhira

Oil portraits created through a traditional Venetian technique, developed slowly through layered painting.

  • 40 × 50 cm — 25,000 MXN — approx. 2 months
  • 90 × 120 cm — 80,000 MXN — approx. 4–6 months

Each portrait is created from photographic references and includes pencil studies as part of the process.

Only a limited number of portraits are accepted each year.
