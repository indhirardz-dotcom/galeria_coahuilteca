Where the experience takes place

Inside the gallery

Most drawing and painting sessions take place inside Galería Coahuilteca, in the historic center of San Miguel de Allende.

Best for graphite, watercolor, charcoal, acrylic, oil pastels, soft pastels and clean mixed media.

Central, easy, walkable and ideal for visitors who want a beautiful creative experience without leaving downtown.

At the rural art studio

Messier and more material-based techniques take place at the private studio in Corralejo de Arriba, a rural community about 15 minutes from the gallery and downtown San Miguel.

Best for oil painting, ceramics and printmaking. This option offers a slower, deeper and more immersive artistic experience surrounded by a quieter landscape.

Transportation is not included, but the location opens easily in Google Maps.
