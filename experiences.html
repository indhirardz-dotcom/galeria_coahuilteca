Choose your technique

  • Acrylic: vibrant color and expressive texture.
  • Watercolor: transparent washes and delicate atmospheres.
  • Oil Pastels: rich, creamy color for expressive strokes.
  • Soft Pastels: bright pigment and painterly effects.
  • Charcoal: dramatic contrast and strong tonal composition.
  • Graphite: precise line, shading and studies.
  • Mixed Media: texture, layers and creative experimentation.

Studio Exclusive

Ceramics, printmaking and oil painting are offered exclusively in the private studio in Corralejo, a short drive from downtown San Miguel de Allende.
